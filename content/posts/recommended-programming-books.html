--- title: "Recommended Programming Books" date: 2020-05-21 tags: [books] ---

Mostly as a note to myself but also something I can point people to who ask me for recommendations on programming books.

They're in no particular order (although they are loosely grouped by subject) and I've purposely not included any reviews or commentary.

I've read or referred to each of the books below multiple times throughout my career and each has proven valuable to me but, like anything, you should evaluate them yourself to see if they're worthwhile for you...

The Phoenix Project

A Novel About IT, DevOps, and Helping Your Business Win

CODE

Clean Code

The Pragmatic Programmer 20th Anniversary Edition

Your Journey To Mastery
